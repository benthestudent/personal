About Blab

Developed to simplify my work in my high school theater, BLab condenses my prior setup of a dozen or more Quicktime Player windows into one graphic interface. With Blab, one can organize, open and play cues with ease. Programmed in Java, BLab stores cue information in an XML project file, saving the cue names, order, file locations and volumes. Upon opening a project, BLab reads the XML data and allows the user to play the various cues as well as modify their attributes or add new cues. Because I programmed BLab quickly and specifically to simplify my needs, the program may not be able to handle all unexpected situations. In the case, that BLab encounters difficulty in a certain situation, please feel free to contact me via email at benthefreelancer@gmail.com
